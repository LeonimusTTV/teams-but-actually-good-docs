Teams but (actually) good works on Chrome based browser and Firefox based browser, so most of the browser are supported by the extension.

<Info>
  Teams but (actually) good never touch the real Teams app, only a Tauri V2 app which just show the Teams website is actually getting modified by the plugins and themes
</Info>

| Browser | Tested ?              | Info                        |
| ------- | --------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Chrome  | <Icon icon="check" /> | -                           |
| Firefox | <Icon icon="check" /> | Need version 128.0 or above |
| Zen     | <Icon icon="check" /> | -                           |
| Safari  | <Icon icon="x" />     | Not compatible              |

To download the extension, heads up on the [website](https://teamsbutactuallygood.dev) and select your browser type, you'll be redirected to the store of your browser (Chrome web store or Firefox Ads-on) to download the extension.
