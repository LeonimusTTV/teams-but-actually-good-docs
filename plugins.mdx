Here you'll have a full tutorial on how to make a plugin for Teams but (actually) good

Firstly something to know is that you shouldn't add your plugins in "src/teams-plugins", since this folder is reserved to official plugin, you should therefore create your own folder called "user-plugins" in "src" ("src/user-plugins"), and here you can work on your plugins

<Note>
  Plugins must have either a "index.ts" or a "index.tsx", this is the entry point of the plugin
</Note>

<Info>
  If you're lost or don't know how to do something, don't hesistate to check on the official plugins works, that might help you a lot
</Info>
