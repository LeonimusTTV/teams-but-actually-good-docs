Teams but (actually) good works on desktop by downloading the downloader for your operating system.

<Info>
  Teams but (actually) good never touch the real Teams app, only a Tauri V2 app which just show the Teams website is actually getting modified by the plugins and themes.
</Info>

| Operating System | Works ? | Info |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Windows | <Icon icon="check" /> | Works without any issues. |
| Linux | <Icon icon="check" /> | Might not work well on certain distro \+ same issue as MacOS with the Company Portal. |
| MacOS | <Icon icon="check" /> | Not able to connect to account that requires the Company Portal. To fix this you must use the extension, that's the only "good" way to fix that issue |

To download the files, heads up on the [website](https://teamsbutactuallygood.dev/download) and select your operating system, then follow the instruction on the installer.
