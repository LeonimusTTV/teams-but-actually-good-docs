<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is TBAG breaking Microsoft's TOS ?" defaultOpen>
    No. It's a client-side mode, it doesn't affect Teams in any bad way nor does it steal information. Use it at your own judgment in corporate environnements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Teams is broken and won't load">
    Open the extension and press the `Clear` button under `Reset Teams Cache`, that way the extension will reset Teams cache, it should fix most issues. If it doesn't work, that means that TBAG is broken and an [issue](https://github.com/LeonimusTTV/teams-but-actually-good/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) should be made.

    You can also try to clear TBAG's Cache with the `Clear` button under `Clear TBAG Cache`, that might help in some cases

    <Frame>
      ![Tbag Extension Reset Cache](/images/tbag-extension-reset-cache.jpg)
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A plugin seems broken and won't do anything">
    Same as `Teams is broken and won't load`
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Safari extension not working">
    To fix the extension doing nothing on Safari, go to the `Settings` (of Safari) → `Extensions` and locate **Teams but (actually) good**, **disable** it and **re enable** it, after that, the extension should open the docs and Teams itself, if that's the case, it should be now working, otherwise see the section `Teams is broken and won't load` in the FAQ
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
