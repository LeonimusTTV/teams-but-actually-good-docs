<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is TBAG breaking Microsoft's TOS ?" defaultOpen>
    No. It's a client-side mode, it doesn't affect Teams in any bad way nor does it steal information. Use it at your own judgment in corporate environnements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team is broken and won't load">
    Open the extension and press the "Clear" button, that way it'll reset Teams and should fix most issues that way. If it doesn't work, that means that TBAG is broken and an [issue](https://github.com/LeonimusTTV/teams-but-actually-good/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) should be made

    <Frame>
      ![Tbag Extension Reset Cache](/images/tbag-extension-reset-cache.jpg)
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A plugin seems broken and won't do anything">

    <Frame>

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Safari extension not working">
    To fix that, go to the Settings → Extensions and local Teams but (actually) good, disable it and reenable it, after that it should be working fine
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
